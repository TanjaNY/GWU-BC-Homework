Cloudiness

The Earth rotates at a high speed counter-clockwise as viewed from the North Pole. If an object starts at the North Pole and moves toward the equator in a straight-line path, for example, the object will appear to deflect to the right of its intended path because the earth is rotating under it as it moves.
