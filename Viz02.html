Humidity

The air in polar regions is usually around 80% saturated. ... Saturation occurs in air that holds very little water vapor even when the air is relatively dry. As a consequence, the relative humidity is higher at polar regions than at 30o latitude, while the specific humidity is higher at 30o than at polar regions.
